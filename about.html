About

Chasing Blue is a a brand of digital art and illustration based in San Antonio, TX. The brand was created by artist Hayley Nelson in 2016 under the name Audientis. The name was changed to Chasing Blue in 2018.
Chasing Blue specializes primarily in anime-style digital illustrations with a strong focus on light and color.
Artist Hayley Nelson has been drawing professionally for 4 years, exhibiting her artwork online and vending at various events and conventions throughout the state of Texas. At her shows she is generally accompanied by her service dog Blue, for whom the studio is named in part.
Hayley came up with the name "Chasing Blue" from her two beloved pets: Chase, a five year old grey and white tabby, and Blue, a four year old red heeler mix and Hayley's psychiatric service dog.
